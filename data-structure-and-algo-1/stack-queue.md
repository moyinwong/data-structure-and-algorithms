# Module 3. Stacks and Queues

## Stack (Last in, first out, LIFO)

### Operations

- `push()`
- `pop()`
- `peek()/top()`
- `isEmpty()`
- `size()`

## Queue (First in, first out, FIFO)

### Operations

- `enqueue()`
- `dequeue()`
- `peek()`
- `isEmpty()`
- `size()`

## Deque (Double ended queue, remove from both end)

### Operations

- `addFirst()`
- `removeFirst()`
- `addLast()`
- `removeLast()`
- `getFirst()`
- `getLast()`
